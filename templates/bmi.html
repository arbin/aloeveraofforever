Body Mass Index Calculator (BMI)

You probably know your height and weight but do you know your BMI? BMI is a measure for human body shape based on an individual's weight and height. Having too few or too many pounds can lead to health problems. That is why it is important to also know your BMI, which can help you determine whether you are underweight, overweight or obese which put your health at risk. Then you can adjust your eating and exercise routines accordingly.
